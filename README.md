# LinkTeen 🐻 — A Bridge to Excellence

**LinkTeen connects high school students with mentors who actually got into UC Berkeley — and shows them the real path, step by step.**

College admissions advice is everywhere, but most of it is generic: "get a leadership role," "do community service," "take hard classes." LinkTeen replaces checklists with lived experience. Every roadmap on the platform is built from the real high school journey of a real admit — the club they joined in 9th grade, the summer program that changed everything, the project that failed 40 times before it worked, and the essay that came out of it.

The app is built first and foremost around **UC Berkeley**, the #1 public university in the country. But because the UC application is shared, the same framework carries directly to **UCLA** and **UCSD** — every mentor experience includes an explicit note on how it translates.

---

## How it works

**For students:**
1. **Explore** real mentor journeys — visual paths like *Science Olympiad → COSMOS Cluster 5 → Independent Research → UC Berkeley EECS* — searchable and filterable by field (CS, Engineering, Pre-Med, Law, Business, Social Impact, Arts, Research, and Berkeley-specific programs).
2. **Read the full story** behind each journey: what the mentor actually did, what they learned, the time it took, why Berkeley valued it, how it applies to UCLA/UCSD, and 2–3 alternatives if the exact program isn't available to you.
3. **Build a roadmap** by borrowing steps directly from mentor journeys or adding custom milestones — real, specific experiences ("President of Environmental Science Club: grew membership 8 → 45, won a $2K grant"), not vague goals. Drag to reorder, track status, and discuss any milestone with a mentor.
4. **Message mentors** with context-aware conversation starters, and track progress on a dashboard with a Berkeley Ready score, activity feed, and personalized recommendations.

**For mentors:**
Register with your UC school, build your journey experience-by-experience (personal story, why it mattered for Berkeley, UCLA/UCSD applicability), and your profile goes live on Explore — ranked above the sample profiles. Manage your students, filter them by target school, and chat with them from your own Mentor Studio.

## Features

- 🧭 **Role-based onboarding** — separate student and mentor flows
- 🗺️ **Mentor journeys** — 10 fully-written sample journeys across CS, mechanical engineering, law, pre-med, business, design, and climate policy
- 🐻 **Berkeley-first, UC-wide** — every experience annotated for Berkeley specifically and for UCLA/UCSD portability
- ✅ **Roadmap builder** — add from journeys, add custom milestones, drag-and-drop reorder, three-state progress tracking
- 💬 **Two-sided messaging** — student↔mentor chat with pre-filled context ("I'm looking at your experience with X...")
- 📊 **Student dashboard** — milestones, mentors connected, hours logged, roadmap progress, Berkeley Ready score, activity feed, recommendations
- 🎓 **Mentor Studio** — profile stats, journey builder, student roster with school filters, and messages
- 🏷️ **Honest demo data** — all sample mentors and students are clearly badged **DEMO** and never auto-respond; real registered mentors always rank above them
- 📸 **Editable profile photos** for both students and mentors

## Design

Warm, bubbly, professional. Primary `#F48947` (orange) with `#A465E2` (purple) accents on a warm off-white `#FFFBF7` background. Outfit for headings, DM Sans for body, 16px+ rounded corners, and soft layered shadows throughout.
